Pig Stomach

Description

Pig Stomach, also traditionally called Hog Maw, is a hearty Pennsylvania Dutch meal. If you've ever met a Dutchmen, you will know that meat and potatoes are the heart of all their favorite meals. Pig Stomach is a mixture of ground pork, loose sausage, and potatoes, with the pig stomach acting as a casing for the mixture

If thinking about handling a pig's stomach is making you queazy, don't worry. You can still make and enjoy this recipe without using a pig stomach by creating a sausage loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 cleaned pig stomach
  • 4-5 potatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound loose sausage
  • 1/2 pound loose italian sausage
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Soak pig stomach in cold water in the refrigerator several hours before stuffing
  2. Dice onion. Peel potatoes and chop into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, mix onion, potatoes, eggs, meats, and salt and pepper until uniform consistency is achieved.
  4. Pour 1/2 cup water into a roasting pan
  5. Stuff pig stomach with meat mixture, then place in roasting pan
  6. Bake at 350Â° for 2 1/2 hours
